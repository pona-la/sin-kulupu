toki, ale o!

October is coming to the end, maybe with spooky things on the horizon. Is October a quiet time of year for you or do storms cause chaos in your life? Either way, October hasn't been quiet for the toki pona community. Let's have a look!

### Songs:
* jan Opa released a new original song, [utala](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XHo0FVppBM8), as an announcement for more stuff called "lon sijelo lon poka pilin", in December!
* waso Asela translated [I wanna be sedated](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JnmIxe5i-6Y) by the Ramones!
* jan Emi translated [Skyscraper](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0BJZXA-hwP8) by FEX!
* lili Kuwita translated [orchids](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V4JEm-ss34o) by glass beach!

### Videos:
* jan Polijan [vlogged about an theme park](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O8AZr-W2DUA) - it's his biggest vlog yet, and the 100th public video on his channel!
* jan Kekan San [played and streamed Deltarune Chapter 4](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OeVZJuld1sk) - with the entire commentary in toki pona, of course!
* The [VRChat](https://bsky.app/profile/tokiponavr.bsky.social) community uploaded a July [presentation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qvhL7ULiutg) by jan Kekan San, in which he talks about the healing process after a physical injury!
* jan Lakuse uploaded their talk about "suno sama" which they presented [during suno pi toki pona (with subtitles)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SnMJqQZp7x8), and added a [clean luka pona version](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cSqq6Ma1tik) and a [toki pona version](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zI3DlgF_mRM)!

### Stories:
* jan Ali narrated [an original little story](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RFQzw-sjXDQ)!
* jan Siwa wrote a dialogue between 2 speakers as an [intro to toki pona](https://blog.purpureus.net/posts/toki-pona-a-review/) in English for a writing contest!
* soko Sijeni is writing [toki pona yuri](https://sijeni.li.pona.la/sp/lon-insa-pi-ma-tu-1/)!

### Graphics:
* sitelen Kuwi made another appearance with a [Bulbasaur anatomy poster](https://www.reddit.com/r/tokipona/s/V70tg7BNjC), translated by jan Kuwi!
* kulupu Kasuno adapted [a Calvin & Hobbes comic strip](https://www.tumblr.com/cartoonofmilk/797411353891684352/been-learning-toki-pona-and-thought-of-a-funny-bit)!
* jan Pikesike [continues translating](https://bato.to/title/204245-jan-poki-li-kalama-wawa/3838408-vol_1-ch_4) the Bocchi the Rock manga!
* jan Sijali translated [chapter 2 of Nights with a Cat](https://bato.to/title/204758-tenpo-pimeja-la-mi-lon-poka-pi-soweli-suwi/3879563-vol_1-ch_2)!
* jan Talija shared a sticker pack for Signal: [sitelen pona pi kulupu Panke](https://toki.social/@yuvalne/115340050625985042)!

### Other:
* ijo vivi, jan Siwa, jan Ke Tami, jan Simiman, jan Mateja, jan Sepulon and many others have been [writing postcards to each other in sitelen pona](https://liputawa.wordpress.com/), with photos and transcripts getting released slowly. For now, the postcards get organised between adults, on the [German toki pona server](https://discord.gg/6QMrNp3X6n)!
* With the addition of [Czech](https://wasona.com/cs/) by jan Suwala, and [Arabic](https://wasona.com/ar/) by jan Mota, the Wasona course is now available in 10 languages! It is now the most translated fully-fledged toki pona course.
* jan Juna made [a toki pona Wordle for graphical calculators](https://www.reddit.com/r/tokipona/comments/1od94ic/i_made_toki_pona_wordle_on_my_graphing_calculator/) and [shared the code](https://github.com/phiathezeroth/nasa-pi-toki-pona/blob/main/wordle%20in%20toki%20pona)!
* jan Jelomun wrote a [command line script](https://github.com/jeremyreeder/wawa-toki-pona) that transforms Latin alphabet toki pona into sitelen pona and vice versa!
