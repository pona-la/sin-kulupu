toki, ale o!

Not even one month in and people have already been so creative in so many different ways! Quick, let's make a snapshot of as much as we can before the calendar switches to February:

### People talking about things in toki pona
* waso laso [visited the tidepools & aquarium](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8VlM213IBGs) in Monterey!
* jan Tene made [a presentation about Aristotle](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RBpYKMeCdAQ) ([alt link](https://seka.pona.la/w/23psFTc4uRiYuK4GFZA8PE))!
* jan Polijan made a short video about [glittering snow](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zZS6SFvTyPY)!
* jan Jekuso and jan Tepo published a new episode of the [kalama sin podcast](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0KO2AIl8ayw)!

### Discussing toki pona
* waso Kasen [used Lexical Functional Grammar to analyse toki pona's syntax](https://codeberg.org/carbeck/tokisyntax/src/branch/master/main.pdf)!
* soweli Kasin made [a tier list of additional words](https://youtu.be/Z--RClozq64)!
* Noise and Bells made a vlog in Lojban (with some Esperanto, subtitles are available in English) about the Esperanto congress where jan Lakuse and jan Tepo made a presentation about toki pona, and then [sang two toki pona songs live](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jM2Eh7ZMvwI&t=10m54s)!
* lipamanka talked about [how complete semantic fields are in toki pona](
https://youtu.be/aeME9ESLnnk)!
* jan Kisuwi's NAPKIN analysis got used in [a paper](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/applied-mathematics-and-statistics/articles/10.3389/fams.2025.1640488/full?utm_source=F-NTF&utm_medium=EMLX&utm_campaign=PRD_FEOPS_20170000_ARTICLE). It's named after a toki pona phrase (nasin pi kipisi nasin)!

### Resources
* [lipu Kemeka](https://kemeka.pona.la/) is a new dictionary project that shows translations of English words, with already over 750 words!
* jan Opela published several parts of a [course](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=miDAI89iTAE) for intermediate learners. While the texts were human-made, all images and sounds are AI-generated.
* jan Ke Tami [published a vocabulary lesson](https://youtu.be/542EpuzLkd8) for German speakers!
* ilo Peli made [a flashcard tool](https://bexli.dev/ilo-peli/) based on nimi.li!
* mute ante released [version 1 of the linja laso font](https://github.com/muteante/linja-laso)!
* pipi Pipitawa's font [sitelen pi pipi tawa](https://wasokeli.github.io/sp-font-maker/sitelen-pi-pipi-tawa) and kulupu Kjesi Kema's font [linja nasa lili](https://wasokeli.github.io/sp-font-maker/linja-nasa-lili-tan-kulupu-Kijesi-Kema.html) are both new fonts that were made with waso Keli's SP Font Maker. There are well over 125 fonts made with SPFM!

### Texts
* The lipu tenpo team [published a new issue of the magazine](https://liputenpo.org/lipu/nanpa-len/)! Read about Toki Pona infrastructure made in 2025, flags in China you might not know about, dinosaur feathers, the Underground Railroad, and more!
* jan Tumu [published several poems and stories](https://dhooth.neocities.org/musi/)!
* jan Wasopimeja translated [Bk VIII:183-235 Daedalus and Icarus](https://jan-wasopimeja.neocities.org/jan%20Tatalu%20en%20jan%20Ikalu.pdf) from Ovid's Metamorphosis, as well as [Bk VIII:1-80 Scylla decides to betray her city of Megara and 81-151 Scylla, deserted, is changed to a bird](https://jan-wasopimeja.neocities.org/jan%20Sila%20en%20jan%20Mino.pdf)!
* jan Asilu has been blogging regularly, on [January 9](https://mitokipona.bearblog.dev/jan-lili-silu-li-lon-poka-mi-la-ona-li-seme/), [11](https://mitokipona.bearblog.dev/jan-pi-poka-nasin/), [12](https://mitokipona.bearblog.dev/jan-majuna-pi-toki-mute/), [14](https://mitokipona.bearblog.dev/pilin-ike-pi-kasi-suli/), [17](https://mitokipona.bearblog.dev/mama-majuna-ante-toki-pi-toki-aged-mother/), [18](https://mitokipona.bearblog.dev/jan-muwama-pasiku/), [21](https://mitokipona.bearblog.dev/kasi-la-sona-pi-toki-lon-li-kama/), [23](https://mitokipona.bearblog.dev/leko-la-o-sona/), [24](https://mitokipona.bearblog.dev/nasin-musi-pi-jan-lawa/) and [26](https://mitokipona.bearblog.dev/jan-pi-lukin-ala-en-suno/)!

### Events
* Inspired by the Pokemon Yellow translation kit? February is mun pi ante toki, "translation month"! Use the shortest month of the year to concentrate on adapting one creative work to toki pona.
  * Talk about what and how you're going to translate during [tenpo pana presentations](https://discord.com/events/301377942062366741/1417571699787829279/1465058643148800000) on February 1st on the [ma pona pi toki pona](https://discord.com/invite/mapona) Discord server!
  * You can also join others to work on different projects together in [dedicated channels](https://discord.com/channels/301377942062366741/936459528424280134) there or [celebrate ongoing and finished translation works](https://discord.com/events/301377942062366741/1462279807957078037). 
  * At the end, another [tenpo pana live stage event](https://discord.com/events/301377942062366741/1417571699787829279/1475205503385600000) will surely present some highlights!
  * Are you working on translations or discussing them in other places? We'd love to hear from you!
  * Rest assured, upcoming newsletters will share what came out of this!
